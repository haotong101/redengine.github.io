Business Development Manager

The Role

Working at Red Engine, you will be responsible for developing new business, bringing in fresh and exciting accounts to sit alongside our existing clients. You will be responsible for generating new, profitable business by providing solutions, including video production and creative services to new clients, whilst identifying gaps in the market and as a result, broadening the extent of solutions we provide!

Key responsibilities include

  • Organising sales planning and resources to deliver budgeted profit and sales targets, creating a pipeline of business/opportunities.
  • Managing pitches and the development of presentations, ensuring the pitch is tailored specifically to the client's needs whilst representing the agency and its story;
  • Working closely with our in-house agency teams to develop key business solutions;
  • Building strong relationships internally (in house client services and creative teams) and externally with new and existing clients.

Desired Skills and Experience

The successful candidate will have:

  • 3-4 years sales experience with a strategic/consultative approach;
  • A strong understanding of the creative market within the video and creative services space; Entrepreneurial qualities, someone who isn't afraid to be bold and look outside the box!
  • Personality and passion - we want industry professionals and specialists that lead by example and get results!
  • Have a consultative approach and be a strong networker who is fully conversant with the range of products and services the agency provides;
  • Have a clear understanding of the competition (strengths and weaknesses) as well as products and services they provide and how these compare in the market;
  • Have a strong understanding of marketing and PR activity, how to best use the team to support client development;
  • Understanding and experience in video production and creative services will be highly regarded.

What's on offer?

The successful candidate will be rewarded with a competitive package and the opportunity to become a key member in a fast growing creative services agency. If you want to work for an ambitious agency with big plans to grow over the next 18 months, then apply now or get in touch for more info!
