PUBLISHING

The Red Engine team has a wealth of experience in publishing and print. From design and creative direction through to print production and management - their knowledge is broad and well renowned.

Our wide ranging experience and expertise offers clients end-to-end technical expertise with a team who are able to take any project from initial concept, through design, content creation, production, into print (and, if required, seamlessly online).

Whether it's magazines, books, brochures or catalogue... publishing content about food, finance music or travel... we have the knowledge and expertise to produce the best end product for you.

That's why our latest collaboration with Iconic Images is such a good pairing. 'Bowie by O'Neill' is the product of this joint venture. We've created a luxury, fine-art book of Terry O'Neill's photography archive of David Bowie, featuring largely unseen images. Both companies are very proud and excited.

This addition to the Red Engine Media and Iconic Images portfolios is to be published in September 2016 - a unique collector's edition, limited to just 500 copies.

For more information or to pre-order please visit bowiebyoneill.com
