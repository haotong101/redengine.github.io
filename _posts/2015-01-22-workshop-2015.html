Don't tell me...show me!



Joy, pleasure, beauty... you can be the best copywriter in the world but a picture tells a thousand words. Visual content is a much underestimated and powerful marketing tool to build and maintain brand and business. With the demand for visual content set to increase further in 2015, Red Engine is launching "The Recipe for visual content success workshop". Each session will be prepared to the exact needs and tastes of your unique products and target audience.

The ultimate aim is to help you to develop a cost effective and manageable content strategy. It will explore how to create meaningful content which can be used across all of today's multiple channels, is on brand, economic and above all, be relevant to your customer. The workshops will explore case study examples to ignite the creative process and are based on the principles of 'COPE' - Create Once and Publish Everywhere.
