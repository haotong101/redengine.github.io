We've also been out on the golf course, here's Keith's summary...

The brief was to shoot a golf course in a day, in late autumn during a week of driving rain. And don't forget to capture the sunrise. And by the way you also need to use a drone - no problem!!!



Our well-behaved drone did us proud, despite its preference for only going out on high summer days, in light winds.

Our key elements for success were having a patient professional golfer who could shoot on demand again, and again and again... Carrying out a comprehensive recce to pre select our holes - you can't make it round all 18 holes on an October day, unless you fancy a spot of night golf. Although you can (and we did) get some great interior shots once the sun went down, in the clubhouse - it would have been rude not to, obviously.

And finally, we warned all the club members that we would be out and about armed with cameras, a drone and likely to be standing in their way whenever they lined up that perfect shot.

It was all good, a success from sun up until sun down. Above are a few screen snaps as a taster, until we can share the completed video with you.
